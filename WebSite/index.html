GPS tracker

Realtime GSM+GPS Tracking of Vehicles Using Arduino an AI thinker

The Idea

A hidden car tracker.

In these days, you often hear on the radio how may cars get stolen. These news make us worried and we are afraid to leave our car parked even in trusted or untrusted locations. The idea for this project is to create a prototype for a GPS tracker which can be used to track our car, as it sends back the exact GPS location of the car by using the GSM network. This prototype can't protect our car from getting stolen, but using this technology, the car can be tracked in real time.
The main goal is to get back the exact location of the device/car by sending an SMS to it, and the GPS tracker uses the GPS and GSM network will determine the exact location and send it back to a specific phone number. It will help to find the stolen or lost car by the GPS position.

The plan

Using Arduino Uno and AI Thinker (A7) GSM & GPS module to build real time vehicle tracker system prototype, to find a car if it gets stolen. Most of the time, when you search on the web to find an idea to make your own project, you can find many successful, or at least, working projects. We got our idea from the following website Where is my car.
This team uses more expensive GSM & GPS module, and it seems they successfuly built the prototype. We are using different parts to build our prototype, because the same parts are not available or are difficult to get. Our team used AI Thinker GSM & GPS module, since it seems easy to get it to work. AI Thinker test
The guy in the video gets very good GPS signal and positions with an external antenna. So, we decided to use this module with an external antenna to our prototype. At the end, if we can get good GPS position from the prototype, as an extra to the project, we could build a sigle web page to display the actual position of the device.

The result

Finnaly the GPS tracker prototype was created. This prototype will allow you to receive a SMS message and respond to a specific phone number with a SMS message. The response SMS message should contain the actual GPS location of the prototype, that is hidden in the car somewhere.

The problem.

The AI Thinker SMS, phone call and GPS functions are working, but the GPS locations are not fixed at all. We got all the required functionalities working in the module, to make the project a success, but the GPS location returned by the AI Thinker module are not just not good enough to determine the location of the vehicle, it is completely unusable.

To get the actual GPS position, different types of hardware can produce different results, and it seems the GPS antenna is not the best to work together with the Ai Thinker A7 module. But the good news is, we are not alone ! After we get the unusable GPS result from the AI Tinker, the required problem solving process helps us to find the wrong side of the module as well. Problem with AI Thinker and GPS location problem
Our team went through all of the possible problem solving processes, that we could find on the Internet, but the result didn't get any better. Because the previous projects got it working correctly, as the possible solution, a different GPS antenna or a signal booster should help find better GPS signal.
